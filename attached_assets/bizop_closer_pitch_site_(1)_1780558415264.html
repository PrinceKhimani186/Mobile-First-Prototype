Mobile Games Are Not Just Games. They Are Digital Products.

Popular mobile games monetize through engagement, in-app purchases, ad networks, upgrades, and ongoing player activity across global app marketplaces.

1

People Download

Mobile apps reach users through global app stores. A free game can be distributed broadly if it is approved and promoted correctly.

2

People Engage

Games create repeat sessions. More engagement can create more chances for ads, upgrades, and user activity.

3

Apps Monetize

Games may monetize through in-app ads, in-app purchases, rewarded ads, upgrades, coins, levels, and premium features.
