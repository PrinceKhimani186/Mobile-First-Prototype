PRIORITY VISUAL HIERARCHY

Not all sections should be treated equally.

The following sections are the most important sections in the entire presentation and should receive the largest amount of visual space, strongest design treatment, and most premium presentation styling.

PRIORITY #1

CONSUMER VS OWNER

Purpose:

Identity shift.

This is where the prospect stops seeing themselves as a consumer and starts imagining themselves as an owner.

Requirements:

* Large visual comparison
* Full-width layout
* Strong iconography
* High contrast
* Dramatic typography
* Premium animations

This should be one of the most memorable sections on the page.

---

PRIORITY #2

THE APP ECONOMY

Purpose:

Build certainty in the industry.

This section should feel like a keynote presentation.

Requirements:

* Large Sensor Tower screenshots
* Category rankings
* Market intelligence visuals
* Download examples
* App store ecosystem graphics

The closer should be able to spend 10+ minutes in this section.

This should be one of the largest sections on the page.

---

PRIORITY #3

WHICH PATH FITS YOU?

Purpose:

Transition from belief to commitment.

Requirements:

* Large package cards
* Premium styling
* Strong visual differentiation
* Accelerator highlighted as Most Popular
* Empire highlighted as Premium

This section should feel like the decision point of the presentation.

---

SECONDARY SECTIONS

* Video Presentation
* How Apps Monetize
* Why Most People Never Enter
* App Squad Solution
* Game Templates
* What Happens Next

These sections should support the presentation but should not visually overpower the three priority sections above.

FINAL DESIGN OBJECTIVE

The page should feel less like a website and more like a cinematic high-ticket sales presentation where the prospect is guided through:

Consumer
↓
Owner
↓
Industry
↓
Possibility
↓
Solution
↓
Decision
↓
Enrollment

Every scroll should increase certainty and emotional engagement.
